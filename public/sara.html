Project Overview

Scheduling Algorithms Development

Developed a scalable scheduling engine, SARA, powered by a multi-agent AI architecture (MAS) and designed to optimise processing times in complex manufacturing environments. Built with Python, SARA efficiently schedules over 20,000 jobs within 10 minutes.

Unlike conventional off-the-shelf scheduling tools, SARA offers exceptional adaptability to industry-specific constraints in sectors such as semiconductors, PCBs, and machine tools. Standard solutions often fall short when handling the custom logic and process rules found in real-world factories.

While some competitors may outperform under simplified workloads, SARA delivers superior flexibility and reliability in complex scenarios to accommodate a wide range of shop structures, achieving a strong balance between performance and customisability. It serves as a robust foundation for digital transformation and production automation in modern manufacturing.

Technical Implementation

SARA's architecture leverages a sophisticated multi-agent system, with each agent specialising in a distinct aspect of the scheduling process. This distributed design enables parallel handling of constraints and optimisation objectives, achieving both high computational performance and exceptional adaptability across diverse manufacturing scenarios. Additionally, its parallel processing structure ensures efficient handling of large datasets and supports scalable performance with near-linear growth as workload demands increase.
